Game of Three

How to Play:

1. Click "Start Game" to begin the game.

2. Once the game starts, you can take turns making moves by clicking on the buttons.

3. The goal is to reach a total of 1 by dividing the current number by 3, rounding if necessary, and choosing -1, 0, or 1 as the next move.

4. The game ends when a player reaches the target number of 1.

5. Enjoy playing!
